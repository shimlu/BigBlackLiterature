About Me as a Reader

Welcome to my literary portfolio. As a student of Great Books, I have embarked on a transformative journey through the timeless narratives that have shaped human consciousness across cultures and eras. This website serves as a testament to my growth as a critical reader, analytical thinker, and engaged interpreter of literature.

Throughout this course, I have developed the ability to approach texts not merely as stories, but as complex cultural artifacts that reveal profound truths about human nature, society, and the human condition. Each work studied has challenged me to think beyond surface narratives and explore the deeper meanings embedded within literary masterpieces.

The Great Books course is an intensive exploration of canonical and contemporary literature that has profoundly influenced human thought and culture. Through careful study of various texts from ancient myths to modern short stories, we have engaged with fundamental questions about identity, power, love, death, and the nature of human existence.

Purpose of This Portfolio

This portfolio serves as a comprehensive showcase of my literary analyses developed throughout the semester. Each analysis demonstrates my ability to apply various critical lenses to different texts, revealing how theoretical frameworks can illuminate hidden meanings, cultural contexts, and universal human experiences embedded within literature.

The website contains seven distinct literary analyses, each employing a different critical approach, along with a synthesis of my learning and a personal reflection on how this course has transformed my understanding of literature and its role in shaping human values and worldviews.

Through this journey, I have learned that literature is not merely entertainment or aesthetic expression it is a powerful medium for understanding ourselves, our societies, and the complex web of human relationships that define our existence. Each critical lens offers a unique window into these truths, and together they form a comprehensive toolkit for engaging meaningfully with texts.
